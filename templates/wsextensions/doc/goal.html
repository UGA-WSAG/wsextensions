The Service Suggestion Engine (SSE) allows you to specify a goal that suggested tools should fulfill. This goal can be expressed in two ways:

  1. A simple description of what you are trying to do/find (e.g., "multiple sequence alignment" or "phylogentic protein distance").
  2. An IRI or label of a known term in an ontology (e.g., "compute protein evolution distance objective" or "http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/OBIws_0000180").
